# Cinnamon Rolls Recipe

## Ingredients for Dough
- bread flour
- caster sugar
- fine salt
- instant dried yeast
- egg
- milk
- unsalted butter

## Cinnamon-Sugar Filling
- unsalted butter
- soft brown sugar or light muscovado sugar
- ground cinnamon
- fine salt

## Icing
- icing sugar
- milk
- unsalted butter

## Instructions
1. Mix together wet ingredients (milk, egg).
2. Combine dry ingredients in a separate bowl.
3. Add yeast to the wet mixture and let it sit for 5 minutes.
4. Combine wet and dry ingredients, then knead in butter until fully incorporated.
5. Roll out dough into a rectangle shape.
6. Spread softened unsalted butter over the entire surface of the rolled-out dough.
7. Sprinkle the cinnamon-sugar filling evenly over the buttered surface.
8. Roll up the rectangle tightly from the short end to form a log, then cut into 9 equal pieces.
9. Arrange the pieces in a greased 13x9 inch baking pan, cut side down.
10. Allow the dough to rise for 1-1.5 hours in a warm place or oven with light on.
